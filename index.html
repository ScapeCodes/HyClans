Plugin Features

Feature Description
Territory Conquest Claim chunks of the world. Build customizable defenses and tax players who pass through your lands.
Player Economy Dynamic marketplaces driven entirely by supply and demand.
Class Systems Master 4 unique combat classes with custom skill trees.
Dungeons & Raids Venture into the deep zones for legendary loot, but beware of rival clans lurking in the shadows.
Clan Alliances Form alliances with other clans for mutual protection and shared resources.
Custom Structures Build unique clan structures with special abilities and upgrades.
Warfare System Declare wars, siege enemy territories, and engage in epic clan battles.
Resource Management Gather, craft, and manage resources for clan progression.
